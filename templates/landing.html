Core Features

Unified Scoring

Comprehensive analysis combining Content, UX, SEO, Social, and Ad Experience into a single weighted score.

Readability Multi-Index

Advanced text analysis using Flesch, Fog, SMOG, Coleman-Liau, and ARI indices to ensure your content is easy to read.

Engagement Heuristics

Analyzes questions, CTAs, and sentiment to maximize reader engagement and interaction.

Informativeness Depth

Evaluates content depth through word count, header structure, media usage, and citation patterns.

Layout Quality

Checks for mobile responsiveness, security, whitespace usage, typography, and contrast ratios.

SEO Optimization

Deep dive into title/meta tags, indexing, schema markup, keyword density, and URL structure.

Ad Intrusiveness

Detects and scores ad density, placement, and autoplay behavior to ensure a good user experience.

Prioritized Recommendations

Actionable advice prioritized by impact: Critical, High, Medium, Low, and Excellent.
